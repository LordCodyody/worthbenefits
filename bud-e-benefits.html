Provided by Several Vetted Underwriters with Years of Experience

Minimum Essential Coverage (MEC)

Minimum Essential Coverage (MEC) satisfies the basic requirements of the Affordable Care Act (ACA) by providing preventive and wellness services. It’s an affordable option that helps employers stay compliant while giving employees access to essential healthcare, such as annual checkups, screenings, and vaccinations.

Sample of Services:

  • Annual wellness exams
  • Preventive screenings (blood pressure, cholesterol, diabetes)
  • Immunizations and vaccines
  • Women’s health services (pap smears, mammograms)
  • Tobacco cessation programs
  • Routine lab work
  • Behavioral health assessments
  • Preventive pediatric care

Minimum Value Plan (MVP)

A Minimum Value Plan (MVP) goes beyond basic preventive care to include comprehensive medical coverage, such as doctor visits, hospitalization, and prescription benefits. It meets ACA standards for minimum value, offering employees more robust protection and helping employers remain fully compliant with federal requirements.

Sample of Services:

  • Primary care and specialist visits
  • Urgent care and emergency services
  • Hospitalization and surgery
  • Diagnostic testing and imaging (x-ray, MRI, CT scan)
  • Prescription drug coverage
  • Preventive and wellness services
  • Mental health and substance abuse treatment
  • Maternity and newborn care

ACA options are not provided by ON Health.

Best Practices for Smarter Benefits Planning

Understand Benefit Implementation Strategies

Reduce or eliminate tax liability through a detailed evaluation of your current benefit options and contribution structures.

Maximize Employee Participation

Encouraging higher participation in your benefits program not only boosts employee satisfaction but also helps extend tenure and improve overall efficiency.

High turnover can be expensive. Companies offering comprehensive benefits see employees stay longer. Workers enrolled in benefits remain up to 47% longer, translating into measurable revenue gains.
